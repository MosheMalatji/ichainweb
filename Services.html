Services

  • Physical Asset Verification 
    The physical asset verification forms the basis for any future asset registers and, as such, a thorough job needs to be done to ensure a solid foundation for the future.
  • Data Gathering 
    We specialise in building and maintaining your asset data, ensuring quality control and data integrity checks throughout the process 
  • Barcoding 
    We provide various types of barcodes to suit each client’s environment and requirements. 
  • Asset Condition Assessment 
    The asset condition will be determined as per the scaling in the Asset Management Guide 2008 from National Treasury. This is based on a 5 level scale namely; Very Poor/Broken, Poor, Fair, Good and Very Good/Excellent. The condition grading will be used in the calculation of the remaining useful lives of assets. The useful life review effect will be calculated prospectively unless if there is compelling evidence to reopen the prior year
  • Reconciliation 
    Reconciliation of specific GL control accounts to determine whether transactions for fixed assets have taken place in these accounts. If necessary, journal entries will have to be made to transfer these assets to the correct asset control accounts.
  • Fair Market Valuation (FMVs)
    Fair market valuation of irreconcilable assets to the Fixed asset register based on condition assessment
  • GRAP/PFMA Financial Tasks 
    GAAP/GRAP/MFMA/PFMA/IFRS/SARBOX Financial Tasks - Our qualified reconciliation and financial experts can assist with all financial tasks and specific requirements for your financials.
  • Impairment Testing 
    Assets will be impaired when it’s found that an assets carrying amount (amount at which an asset is recognised in statement of financial performance) exceeds its recoverable amount (RA).
